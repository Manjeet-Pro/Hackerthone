HTML Nested Lists & Tables: The Complete Guide

Lecture 1 Lecture 2 Lecture 3 Lecture 4 Lecture 5 Lecture 6

1. Nested Unordered Lists

A nested unordered list is a list within a list using <ul> inside <li>.

  • Fruits
    • Apple
    • Banana
    • Orange
  • Vegetables
    • Carrot
    • Broccoli
    • Spinach
  • Dairy

2. Nested Ordered Lists

A nested ordered list is a numbered list inside a numbered list using <ol> inside <li>.

  1. Prepare ingredients
    1. Wash vegetables
    2. Cut fruits
  2. Cook food
    1. Boil water
    2. Saute vegetables
  3. Serve meal

3. Mixed Nested Lists

You can mix ordered lists inside unordered lists and vice versa.

  • Steps to Bake a Cake
    1. Preheat oven
    2. Mix ingredients
    3. Bake for 30 minutes
  • Decoration
